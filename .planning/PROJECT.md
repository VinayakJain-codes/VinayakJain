# 🧑‍💻 Vinayak Jain — Portfolio Website Plan

## What This Is
A personal portfolio website for Vinayak Jain, a Full-Stack Developer, Builder, and Creator under the brand Vicinix.
The goal is to showcase his skills, past projects, and services, acting as a "builder's journal" rather than a generic portfolio, and enabling a professional online presence.

## Core Value
A premium, developer-meets-creator aesthetic that establishes trust and highlights robust full-stack capabilities, particularly focusing on Next.js, Supabase, and Tailwind.

## The Architecture
**Tech Stack:**
- **Frontend**: Next.js 14 (App Router), React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Backend/DB**: Supabase, PostgreSQL, RLS, PostGIS, Realtime
- **Deployment**: Vercel (free tier)

**Design Direction:**
- **Theme:** Dark base (`#0a0a0a`), orange accent (`#f97316`), green pop (`#13ec5b`)
- **Typography:** Display: `Syne` or `Space Grotesk` · Body: `DM Mono` or `Geist Mono`
- **Motion:** Subtle scroll reveals, cursor glow, section transitions
- Responsive mobile plan (stacked cards, tap limits, burger nav, no horizontal scroll)
